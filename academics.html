About Us

Academics

Learning is our core purpose at St. Peters School where we adopt a child-centered approach.

Choosing the right school is a pivotal moment in your child's life. It sets the tone for their academic and personal growth, shaping who they become as learners and individuals. At St. Peters School, we recognize the significance of this decision and strive to provide an educational experience that nurtures our students' potential in every facet of their lives.

Check out our curriculum and how we organize learning at the various stages

At St. Peters School, we understand that choosing a Creche for your child is an important decision, as it marks the beginning of their educational journey. Our Creche stage, which comprises of children aged three months to four years old, is split into three sub-stages: Creche, Pre-Kindergarten, and Kindergarten. Each stage is designed to cater to the unique needs of your child, ensuring they receive the best possible start to their education. Our Creche program is specially designed to meet the needs of infants aged three months to one year old.

We provide a nurturing and safe environment for your child to learn and grow. Our qualified staff members are dedicated to providing personalized care to each child, ensuring they receive the attention and support they need at this early stage in their development. We understand that infants require a lot of attention and care, which is why our staff-to-child ratio is kept low to ensure your child's needs are met.

The elementary stage is a crucial time in a child's academic journey, where they develop a solid foundation of knowledge and skills that will serve them well throughout their academic and personal life. At our school, we understand the importance of this stage, and we are committed to providing our students with the best possible learning environment and educational experience. Our elementary program is designed to cater to the unique needs of children aged 5 to 10 years old, ensuring that they receive a well-rounded education that covers all aspects of their development.

We have a team of highly qualified and experienced teachers who are dedicated to ensuring that our students receive the highest quality of education and care. Our teachers are passionate about their work and are committed to helping each child reach their full potential. The elementary stage of our school provides children aged 5 to 10 years old with a well-rounded education that caters to their academic, social, and emotional development. Our program is designed to be fun, engaging, and challenging, and we are committed to helping each child reach their full potential. We believe that every child deserves an education that prepares them for success in the real world, and we are proud to offer such an education to the young learners in our community.

Our secondary school program is designed to provide students with a challenging and supportive learning environment that prepares them for success in college and beyond. At this stage, our students are maturing both academically and socially. We recognize the importance of building strong relationships with our students, which is why our faculty members work closely with each student to ensure that they receive individualized attention and support. Our experienced teachers are committed to helping each student reach their full potential and excel in their academics.

In our secondary school, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes a range of subjects, including mathematics, science, history, English, and foreign languages. Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in these core subjects, while also allowing them to explore their interests through a variety of elective courses. Our students are encouraged to take advanced courses to challenge themselves academically, and we offer a variety of opportunities for them to pursue their passions, such as our clubs, societies, and sports teams.
