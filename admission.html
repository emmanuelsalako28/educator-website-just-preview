Admission

Choosing the right school is a pivotal moment in your child's life. It sets the tone for their academic and personal growth, shaping who they become as learners and individuals. At St. Peters School, we recognize the significance of this decision and strive to provide an educational experience that nurtures our students' potential in every facet of their lives.


As a co-educational school with over five years of experience, we understand the nuances of how boys and girls learn and grow. Our well-rounded approach to education emphasizes excellence in academics, athletics, arts, and citizenship. We believe that a comprehensive education that fosters personal integrity and leadership skills is the key to success, both inside and outside the classroom.
